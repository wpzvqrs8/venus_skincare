CO2 Fractional Machine

CO2 Fractional laser is the most advanced conceptual fractional CO2 skin peeling laser system with a wavelength of 10600nm. In addition to its fine skin-peeling effect, it can effectively penetrate the laser beam into the dermis. It is the most beneficial skin recovery system and can achieve the long-term effect of collagen remodeling as well as improvement of aged skin conditions due to exposure to light. It can be very safely applied to various types of scars compared to existing 100% total layer skin peeling lasers. In addition, it does not require a long period of recovery or side effects. It can remodel various types of deformed scars and skin tissue effectively.

Application:

  • 1. Acne scars, Actinic keratoses
  • 2. Skin cutting: for warts removal, naevus removal, acrochordon removal, polypus removal
  • 3. Basal cell carcinoma
  • 4. Burn debridement
  • 5. Laser skin resurfacing
  • 6. Eliminate the scar: Traumatism, operation
  • 7. Improve texture tone and pore size
  • 8. Erase unwanted brown spots
  • 9. Smooth wrinkles around the eyes and mouth

Advantage:

  • 1. Ultrapulse- High energy, short pulse breath. less pain, less damage.
  • 2. CPG (Central Pattern Generator)- Make the dots diameter and interval average.
  • 3. 3 kinds of probes- Fractional probe, Magic probe, Gynae probe.
