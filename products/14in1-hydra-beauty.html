Multifunctional 14 In 1 Hydra Beauty Facial Machine

Hydra facial is a non-invasive treatment that builds healthy skin for the long term and provides immediate, noticeable results in as little as 30 minutes. It is a 3-step treatment that removes dead skin cells and extracts impurities while simultaneously bathing the new skin with cleansing, hydrating and moisturizing serums. The treatment is soothing, refreshing, non-irritating and immediately effective.

Hydra dermabrasion is an all natural, virtually painless, skin treatment procedure that dramatically improves the appearance and texture of your skin in a rapid manner and provides a discernible enhancement to the recipient. Hydro dermabrasion treatments are highly effective in skin texture improvement, wrinkle reduction, removal of unwanted pigmentation and overall skin rejuvenation. It is also very useful with acne, blackheads and other comedone eruptions as it quickly and effortlessly removes them without any pain or discomfort.

14 in 1 Hydra Facial Machine is equipped with 14 probes: Hydro dermabrasion, Diamond peeling, Spray Gun, Ultrasonic for face, Ultrasonic for eyes, Bio, Skin Scrubber, High frequency, PDT Light, Photon bio brush x 2, Vacuum Pen, Cold Hammer, Dermapen.

It can solve various skin problems such as deep cleansing, moisturizing, whitening, acne, blackheads, wrinkles, oily skin, lifting, firming, sensitive skin, etc. The 14 in 1 hydra facial machine is suitable for all skin types and improves skin texture and tone.
