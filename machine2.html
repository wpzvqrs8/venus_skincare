FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How many sessions are needed?

Typical treatment courses consist of 6-10 sessions, performed weekly or bi-weekly depending on the treatment area and desired results.

Is the treatment painful?

Venus Elite treatments are comfortable and often described as feeling like a warm massage. No anesthesia is required and there is no downtime.

When will results be visible?

Many patients see initial results after 3-4 sessions. Optimal results are typically achieved after completing the full treatment course.

What maintenance is required?

After the initial treatment course, maintenance sessions every 4-6 weeks are recommended to sustain results.
